About Us

Since 2000, Food Trip Restaurant has been serving up Mediterranean and American cuisine to the Georgetown and surrounding areas. Food Trip's has become a destination of Georgetown locals as well as international travelers from around the country seeking authentic Middle Eastern cuisine. Food Trip’s provides great food in a warm and welcoming environment with an unique atomosphere.

Come experience for yourself why Food Trip’s is the perfect blend of taste, service, and price.
